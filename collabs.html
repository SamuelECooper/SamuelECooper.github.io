Collaborators

Outside of my work with Dr. Joseph Dunsmoor and in his lab, I collaborate on a variety of projects with other scientists. I continue to work with my primary graduate school adviser, Dr. Shmuel Lissek, and his graduate students, as well as with my secondary adviser, Dr. Robert Krueger. More recent collaborations include structural psychopathology work with Drs. David Watson and Sara O’Brien, biobehavioral trait measurement and prediction with Dr. Chris Patrick and Emily Perkins, and forays into affiliated fields, including global mental health with Dr. Dan Lakin. I am also a member of the Neurobiological Foundations and Quantitative Methods workgroups within the HiTOP consortium.
